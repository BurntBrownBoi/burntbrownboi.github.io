About Section

Here, you can learn more about me and my skills.

Specializing in Game Development, my skill set includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Lua and through my internship at the Cincinnati Insurance Companies, I discovered object-oriented programming in C#, TypeScript, XML, and more via custom Angular Apps. Growing up, I've always played racing games ranging from Gran Turismo 4 (Absolute GOAT), Forza Horizon 3 which was a well optimized masterpiece for its' time, and just about every need for speed game in existence. I've always had a keen eye for how every element in a game is created. From the level design to pixel-peeping at distant objects and learning about render distance on my own and how certain optimizations are made just by playing through the game multiple times. Currently in Roblox Studio, I am working on two separate games. A simple shooter that follows the trend of "unfinished" or "untitled" games and is nearing completion. The other is an open-world racing game to rival other games on Roblox by having the highest visual fidelity and stability. I already have a name, Veloce! I am also a part-time simracer on the weekends, while not official the goal is to be part of a team at some point via my weekend races. My love for cars has always tied in with my passion for video games and hopefully, once I finish making Veloce, I will have made a racing game that not only pushes Roblox's graphical engine but is well-made and allows car enthusaists everywhere to enjoy an awesome virtual world.
